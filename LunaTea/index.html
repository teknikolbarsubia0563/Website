why choose us?

Tea has enchanted Ms. Sarah Nicole Barsubia since childhood, evolving beyond a mere drink during cherished moments with her grandmother. From serving in a toy teahouse to navigating her parents' businesses, Sarah discovered tea's infinite realms—spirituality, wellness, and serenity.

Driven by a natural inclination, she embarked on a magical journey of service, sharing the wonders of tea with enthusiasts worldwide. Every moment is a blend of learning and growth, making Sarah's life a captivating tale spun from the threads of her enduring love for tea.
